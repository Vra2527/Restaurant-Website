About Our Bay Leaf Restaurant

Bay Leaf offers a unique culinary experience with dishes rooted in the universal classics and prepared with authentic flair.

HISTORY

Philosophy

Bay Leaf was originally opened in 2005, housed in a historic space on Adayar Street.Owner Nedumaran was driven by his passion for incredible food and the hospitality industry. The restaurant gave Nedumaran a home to work from while still focusing on Indian Food, a culinary corner he has long excelled in, while also establishing his local restaurants– Bay Leaf and Timber Street Meats– as pillars . Bay Leaf is located in Adayar’s revitalized West Street. We welcome our guests to join us and take advantage of all that we have to offer from our talented crew in our kitchen. Our shareable menu is designed to provide a wondrous variety of possible combinations. We shy away from the traditional course meal, in favor of a small and large plate shareable experience curated by our servers and chefs
